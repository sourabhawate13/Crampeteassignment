Fourth Question Answer

If you have an active Individual Membership and purchase another one, the new subscription term will be added to the time remaining on your current term. For example, if you have 35 days remaining on your Individual Membership and buy a 365-day Individual Membership, your remaining subscription term will now be 400 days. You can add Individual Memberships up to a total term of three years. Note that you cannot combine Family Memberships and Individual Memberships
